Summary0011870: Name of Age is Partially Missing
DescriptionAfter generating a world with two great powers remaining, one died and stayed dead. The other died and was revived by a necromancer.

This should have led to an "Age of (Marsh Titan Name)" but instead led to "Age of ___" The description for this age also reads: "The Age of was a time when the marsh titan was the only great power in the world."

I believe the necromancer's specific type of resurrection may have erased the titan's name, thereby affecting the name of the Age.
