Summary0010346: NPCs demanding other characters identify themselves in a loop
DescriptionWhile trying out the new kobold site system I ran across an annoying bug. The non-historical characters in the site (I think) keep on asking for the identify of eachother, but no identities are ever given in return. That is probably because the characters are non-historical so they do not have an identity to give resulting in an eternal loop.
Steps To Reproduce1. Load up an adventurer site.
2. Pay attention to the conversation screen.

 Still happening, but I think I found something. I think the people asking for identities are outcasts, since I had one night creature 'outcast' in my site and they were the only one who were asking for identity constantly.

Basically the way it is programmed is that those resident on the site ask those who do not belong to their group for identity. The AI reciever is then programmed to lie and create a new fake identity belonging to the asker's civ. However in this case, the outcast does not have a civ that anyone can pretend to belong to, so the dialogue gets stuck.
